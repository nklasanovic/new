About me

Since my childhood, I was into computers and games. Especially games. These two became something very special to me. I remember clearly, like it was yesterday. Me at the age of six, one of those cold winter nights and Max Payne in bullet-time "doing his thing" on my monitor. Such a precious memory of mine. Also, turned out it became my favorite game of all time. Because it contains the best things in gaming for me. Engaging gameplay combined with well-written & memorable characters and meaningful story. The most important things I look for in games even to this day. Now, I tend to play all sorts of genres and I enjoy every single minute of doing that. Characters from games aren't just some pixels on the screen to me, they're much more than that. Like that Max Payne isn't just some random dude trying to live an American dream.
